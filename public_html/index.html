Sylvania Southview Tri-M

Chapter 8260 est. 2019

Our Purpose

Tri-M, standing for Modern Music Masters, is a student-led volunteer organization whose purpose is to enrich the Greater Toledo area through music! We welcome students from band, choir, as well as orchestra to perform with us in order to both strengthen students' performance skills and give back to our community.

What we've done

Tri-M has performed at Miracle on Main Street, the Manor House, the Sylvania Senior Center, and more! Tri-M also has a long-standing Tradition of providing junior high students with private lessons over the summer, and into the school year.

Why Join Tri-M?

Tri-M is a fun way to earn volunteer hours through performance and fundraising events. Through Tri-M you can collaborate with people from different ensembles more than you usually would. It's also a great way to not only get accustomed to performing with others but in front of an audience as well. Plus, we do some fun social events!

Who Can Join?

Tri-M is NOW open to all Freshman Sophomores, Juniors, and Seniors in Choir, Band, or OrchestrA! Requirements include: a 4.0 GPA average or higher in music classes 3.0 GPA average or higher in Other classes monthly meeting Attendence Volunteer in at least 1 event per quarter Please note that your participation affects officer eligibility in the future!
